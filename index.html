About THC

Making Technology Accessible for Everyone

Touchless Head Control (THC) was born from a simple belief: technology should be accessible to everyone, regardless of physical ability. Our software empowers individuals with limited mobility to use computers independently, breaking down barriers to digital interaction.

Built with cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning, THC transforms your webcam into a powerful input device. Whether you have a temporary injury, a permanent disability, or simply want a hands-free experience — THC is designed for you.
